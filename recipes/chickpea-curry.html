Chickpea Curry

Description

This recipe started out a challenge wondering could we cook a beautiful Chickpea curry in 5 mins – we weren’t sure if we could do it but low and behold it worked and it really tasted so good – so much so the recipe video for this has over 2 million views.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 a red onion
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2tbsp of curry powder
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 x 400g tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 x 400g tin of chickpeas
  • 1 x 400g tin of coconut milk
  • 1 avocado

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil on a medium heat.
  2. Thinly slice the onion and garlic and add to the pan.
  3. Grate the ginger into the pan. No need to remove the skin!
  4. Add the spices and cook for 30 seconds.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, coconut milk and chickpeas.
  6. Add the lime juice, season to taste and serve with avocado and the grain of your choice. Lovely!
